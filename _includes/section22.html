22.2 Reproducing Derek's Single-cell Results

Quality control was performed using FRiP (Fraction of Reads in Peaks) scores, where higher values indicate better data quality. Cells were filtered based on the 5th and 95th percentiles of FRiP scores. Each histone modification was analyzed using tile sizes optimized for its genomic distribution pattern.
