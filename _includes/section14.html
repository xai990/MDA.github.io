14.5 Key factor: PCA dimensions used by WNN

Because the SEACells weight matrices for K4 and K27 are submatrices derived from RNAPII, the effective weights differ from those used for RNAPII itself. In practice, K4 and K27 show greater separation than RNAPII in UMAP. To balance contributions in WNN, we reduced the PCA dimensionality for K4 and K27 relative to RNAPII.
